Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BambooBasics

How long do bamboo toothbrushes last?

Our bamboo toothbrushes are designed to last 2-3 months with regular use, just like traditional plastic toothbrushes. The bamboo handle is naturally antimicrobial and water-resistant, while the biodegradable bristles maintain their effectiveness throughout the recommended usage period.

How does the subscription work?

You'll receive a new bamboo toothbrush every 2-3 months (you choose the frequency). Each delivery includes 1-4 toothbrushes depending on your family size. You can pause, skip, or cancel anytime through your account dashboard. No commitments, just convenience and sustainability.

Are the toothbrushes really eco-friendly?

Absolutely! Our handles are made from sustainably harvested bamboo that biodegrades in 6 months when composted. The bristles are made from plant-based materials that break down naturally. Our packaging is plastic-free, using recycled cardboard and biodegradable mailers.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within the United States and Canada. We're working on expanding to Europe and Australia in 2024. All orders include free shipping, and we use carbon-neutral delivery methods whenever possible to minimize our environmental impact.

Are bamboo toothbrushes as effective as plastic ones?

Yes! Our bamboo toothbrushes are dentist-approved and just as effective at removing plaque and maintaining oral health. The bristles are designed to the same specifications as premium plastic toothbrushes, with medium firmness that's gentle on gums while thoroughly cleaning teeth.

What's the pricing for the subscription?

Our subscription starts at $8/month for a single toothbrush delivered every 3 months ($24 per delivery). Family plans are available with discounts for multiple toothbrushes. Early bird subscribers get 20% off their first box, plus free shipping on all orders. No hidden fees or long-term commitments.
